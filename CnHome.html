About me

Currently I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Michigan State University. I am fortunately advised by Prof. Jiliang Tang and Prof. Hui Liu in the DSE lab

Before that, I worked as a research assistant in the Department of Computing, PolyU and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, CUHK successively.

Formerly, I got a Master's in Applied Statistics (Big Data Analysis) from the School of Data Science in 2021 and a B.A. in Sociology from the Department of Sociology in 2019 both from Fudan University.

My research interests lie in Graph Neural Networks, Natural Language Processing, Causal Inference, as well as their applications in Mass Communication, Sociology of Education and Cultural Politics.
