About me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Michigan State University and a member of the Data Science and Engineering lab. I am fortunately supervised by Prof. Jiliang Tang.
Before joining MSU, I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics and Statistics in Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) in June 2022.
